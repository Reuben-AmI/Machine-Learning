Stochastic Gradient Descent

In this notebook we will implement Stochastic Gradient Descent

Stochastic Gradient Descent is a variation of gradient descent. What makes it different from gradient descent is in this variation, the gradient descent procedure is ran but the update to the coefficients is performed for each training instance, rather than at the end of the batch of instances.

This variation is preffered when dealing with large datasets due to how much quicker learning can occur.
